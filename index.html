About Us


Team History

Founded in 2022 by Rayomnd Favaza and Lee Saucier, the Louisiana Tech Wrestling Club aims to grow wrestling at all ages, but especially the collegiate level in the state of Louisiana, and the southeast as a whole. We welcome men and women, regardless of skill level, to join our team and help us build a strong wrestling community on campus. Members are given the option to compete throught the NCWA in tournaments across the country, or to join the team in a practice only capacity.
